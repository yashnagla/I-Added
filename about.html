How it works?


Discover a Cause

Explore local organizations and learn about the causes they support. Find the perfect match for your intrests and values.


Make a Donation

Donate directly to the organization of your choice. You can also set up recurring donations to provide ongoing support.


Track your impact

Follow the impact of your donations and see how local organizations are using your contributions to make a difference in your community


Celebrate your contribution

Share your contributions on social media and inspire others to join you in supporting local organizations
